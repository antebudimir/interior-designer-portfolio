About Me

Hi and thanks for visiting my website!

I’m a designer by profession and an adventurer by nature. The latter played a major part for me in becoming a designer in the first place, and helps me in advancing my existing knowledges and learning new things today.

I believe that a great design is a prefect balance between all the values of the equation. We live in an imperfect world and making the most out of it makes it perfect for each of us. Same goes with a design project, whether it is making a logo or an office design: timeframe, budget, client’s wishes and expectations, functionality and style, all have to come to a perfect balance. After all, great design is a usable design, be it a product or a space.

Apart from design I’m a big nature lover, cat enthusiast and a kitchen- experimenter. I’m also into making organic care cosmetics and soap- making. Each time some water and oil turns into an emulsion it feels like a little magic of potion-making.

All of that I happily share with my beloved husband Ante. He is a great support and a good critic. Ante is a web developer with a musician’s soul. This beautiful site was developed by him. He also has his music project released, you can learn more about it here: Patterns of Attachment.

We’ve recently moved to Croatia and are open to any cooperation and interesting projects. So, don’t hesitate to contact any of us if you have some interesting ideas.
